# Website Updates Summary  
**Chris, Your Man with a Van**  
**chrisyourmanwithavan.com**  
**Date:** June 2025

---

This document is a plain-language overview of the website improvements completed for your business. It is intended for review, sign-off, and handover — no technical knowledge required.

---

## What We Set Out to Do

The main goals were to:

1. **Help more local customers find you on Google** — especially in towns where visibility was weaker (e.g. Irvine, Kilmarnock, Troon, Ayr).
2. **Make it easier for visitors to contact you** — WhatsApp, Messenger, and phone from any page.
3. **Build trust** — prominently showing your **77 five-star Google reviews**.
4. **Improve the experience on mobile phones** — where most visitors browse.

---

## 1. New Local Pages (SEO)

We added **102 new pages** that combine a **town + service**, for example:

- `/locations/irvine/small-removals`
- `/locations/kilmarnock/courier`
- `/locations/ayr/waste-removal`

These pages target searches like *“man with a van in Irvine”* and *“courier in Kilmarnock”*, giving Google more relevant content for each area you serve.

**17 priority towns** received extra attention (titles, headings, and wording tuned for search):

Irvine, Kilmarnock, Troon, Ayr, Dalry, Ardrossan, Cumnock, Beith, Auchinleck, Darvel, Mauchline, Prestwick, Newmilns, Kirkconnel, Mossblown, Dreghorn, Girvan.

Each town still has its main **location hub page** (e.g. `/locations/irvine`), which now links through to these detailed service pages.

---

## 2. Homepage Improvements

- **Clearer hero section** — title, short description, WhatsApp & Messenger buttons, and your **77 five-star reviews** badge.
- **Shorter, easier-to-read text** on mobile so contact buttons appear without excessive scrolling.
- **“Near me” section** added to target local searches across Ayrshire.
- **Page title and description** updated for better Google listings.
- **FAQ section** enhanced with structured data so Google can show rich results.

---

## 3. Location Pages (Town Hubs)

Each location hub page (e.g. Irvine, Ayr, Kilmarnock) now includes:

- **Updated page titles and descriptions** for search engines.
- **Hero section** with the same background image as the homepage, WhatsApp & Messenger buttons, and the **reviews badge**.
- **Service cards with photos** — each service shows an image, short description, and a simple **“Learn more →”** link.
- **“Why Choose Chris” section** with your photo collage (same images as the About section on the homepage).
- On **mobile**, your photo appears directly under the hero for a personal touch.
- **Reviews section** highlighting **77 five-star Google reviews**, with links to view or leave a review on Google.
- **Cross-links** to nearby towns and related services.

---

## 4. Trust & Social Proof (77 Reviews)

Your **77 five-star Google reviews** are now shown consistently in key places:

| Where | What visitors see |
|--------|-------------------|
| Homepage hero | Google badge with gold stars |
| About Me section | Same reviews badge |
| Location page heroes | Reviews under contact buttons |
| Location reviews section | Full reviews + “77 five-star” messaging |
| Bottom quote areas | Reviews badge on selected pages |

This reinforces credibility before visitors get in touch.

---

## 5. Easier Contact (Sticky Button)

A **floating contact button** appears on the **bottom-right of every page** (mobile and desktop). Tapping it opens:

- **WhatsApp**
- **Facebook Messenger**
- **Call**

Visitors can reach you from any page without scrolling back to the top.

---

## 6. Mobile Experience Fixes

Several mobile issues were addressed:

- **Promo banner** no longer overlaps the main heading.
- **Hero content** laid out in a logical order: title → description → “Get a Free Quote” → buttons → reviews → image.
- **Quote box removed on mobile** — just the green and blue contact buttons (cleaner and faster).
- **Spacing improved** between the reviews badge and the image below it.
- **Decorative arrow** behind “Chris” restored and positioned correctly.
- **CTA buttons** sized and spaced for easy tapping.

---

## 7. Desktop Homepage Layout

The desktop hero was redesigned:

- Content is **left-aligned** (title, text, buttons, reviews).
- The old **boxed “Get a Free Quote” panel** on the right was removed — contact options sit directly under your intro text.
- Content sits **higher on the screen** (not pinned to the bottom).
- The red **arrow graphic** behind “Chris” is in place as before.

---

## 8. Cross-Linking & Navigation

To help visitors (and Google) move around the site:

- **Footer** links to key towns and services.
- **Service pages** link to relevant location pages.
- **Location pages** link to each service in that town and to nearby towns.
- **Sitemap updated** with all new local service URLs (102 additional pages).

---

## 9. Services Hub

The main **Services** page and individual service pages received improved titles and descriptions, especially for high-value searches like end-of-tenancy clearance.

---

## What Was Not Changed

To keep scope clear, the following were **out of scope** for this website work (but remain important for local visibility):

- Google Business Profile posts or products  
- Online citations / directory listings  
- Asking customers in specific areas for more reviews  
- Paid advertising  

These can still help rankings but are separate from the website build.

---

## Technical Notes (For Your Reference)

- Site built with **Astro** and deployed on **Netlify**.
- **Live Google reviews** on the site use your Google Places API when configured on Netlify (`GOOGLE_PLACES_API_KEY`). If the key is not set, the site shows your existing static review examples as a fallback.
- A full **production build** has been run successfully with all new pages included.
