# Upwork Job Post: SEO Audit & Growth Strategy

**Title**: SEO Expert Needed - Complete Website Audit & Growth Strategy for Local Van Services Business

---

## Project Description

I'm looking for an experienced SEO specialist to conduct a comprehensive audit of my van services website and develop a data-driven growth strategy. The site is built with Astro (static site generator), is already live and ranking in Google Search Console, but needs expert analysis to identify opportunities for improvement and accelerate growth.

**Website**: https://chrisyourmanwithavan.com/

**Business Type**: Local business - "Man with a Van" services (removals, courier, waste removal) serving Ayrshire, Scotland

---

## Project Scope

### Phase 1: Comprehensive SEO Audit

**Technical SEO Audit**:
- Review site architecture, URL structure, internal linking
- Analyze page speed and Core Web Vitals
- Check mobile responsiveness and user experience
- Review robots.txt, sitemap.xml, structured data (Schema.org)
- Identify crawlability and indexing issues
- Review canonical URLs and duplicate content issues
- Check for broken links and 404 errors
- Analyze HTTPS/security setup

**On-Page SEO Audit**:
- Review title tags, meta descriptions, H1/H2 structure
- Analyze keyword optimization and density
- Check content quality and uniqueness
- Review image alt tags and optimization
- Analyze content length and keyword coverage
- Review internal linking strategy

**Off-Page SEO Audit**:
- Analyze backlink profile and quality
- Identify local SEO opportunities (Google Business Profile)
- Review citation consistency (NAP - Name, Address, Phone)
- Analyze competitor backlinks and strategies

**Content Audit**:
- Review existing content quality and relevance
- Identify content gaps and opportunities
- Analyze keyword coverage across all pages
- Review location-specific pages (27 location pages currently)
- Check service pages for optimization opportunities

**Local SEO Audit**:
- Review Google Business Profile setup
- Analyze local citation consistency
- Check location-specific landing pages
- Review local schema markup
- Analyze local keyword opportunities

### Phase 2: Competitive Analysis

- Identify top 3-5 competitors in the local market
- Analyze their SEO strategies (keywords, content, backlinks)
- Compare rankings for target keywords
- Identify gaps and opportunities vs. competitors

### Phase 3: Growth Strategy Development

**Immediate Actions (Next 30 Days)**:
- Prioritized list of quick wins
- Critical technical fixes
- Content optimization priorities
- Local SEO improvements

**Short-Term Strategy (1-3 Months)**:
- Keyword targeting strategy
- Content creation plan
- Link building strategy
- Local SEO enhancement plan

**Long-Term Strategy (3-12 Months)**:
- Advanced SEO tactics
- Content expansion roadmap
- Link acquisition strategy
- Conversion optimization recommendations

### Phase 4: Reporting & Recommendations

**Deliverables**:
1. **Comprehensive Audit Report** (PDF/Google Doc) including:
   - Executive summary with key findings
   - Detailed technical SEO findings with priorities
   - On-page optimization recommendations
   - Content gaps and opportunities
   - Local SEO action plan
   - Competitor analysis summary

2. **Action Plan** with:
   - Prioritized list of fixes (High/Medium/Low)
   - Estimated impact for each recommendation
   - Step-by-step implementation guides
   - Timeline for implementation

3. **Growth Strategy Document** including:
   - Keyword targeting strategy
   - Content calendar recommendations
   - Link building opportunities
   - Local SEO enhancement plan
   - Conversion optimization ideas

4. **Presentation/Discussion**:
   - 30-60 minute call to walk through findings
   - Q&A session
   - Implementation guidance
